What is Mai Đào?

Mai Đào: A Vietnamese Cultural Journey main event is an evening-length celebration of dance, music and theatre, winning over a new audience all the while deepening our understanding of the Vietnamese Canadian heritage. Its objective is to nourish the pride of Vietnamese Canadians and empower them as ambassadors beyond the boundaries of their home community.

Moving past symbols like phở and conical hats, it entertains in Engaging participants of all ages and skill levels, from social, school and university groups, Mai Đào also features contributions by the distinguished Khac Chi Bamboo Music Ensemble and the Vancouver Inter-Cultural Orchestra (VICO).
